Digital Real-world Modeling and Ecological Sensitivity Analysis of Urban Parks

Used Phantom 4 Pro V2.0 for UAV tilt photography and Context Capture to construct 3D modeling of the Orchid Garden urban park. Applied ArcGIS to perform ecological sensitivity analysis of real-time park data, integrated with the 3D model using InVEST and Python-based statistical analysis.
