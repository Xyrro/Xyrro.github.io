Glimpse3D

Real-time 3D mesh generation from a single webcam image. Point your webcam at an object, click capture, and get a textured 3D mesh you can orbit, zoom, and pan. Powered by TripoSR for single-view 3D reconstruction, with background removal via rembg/U2Net and interactive rendering using Three.js.
